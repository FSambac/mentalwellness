How To Meditate 🧘

Simple Beginner Meditation

1️⃣ Sit comfortably.

2️⃣ Relax your shoulders.

3️⃣ Close your eyes gently.

4️⃣ Focus on your breathing.

5️⃣ Inhale deeply through your nose.

6️⃣ Exhale slowly through your mouth.

7️⃣ Continue for 5 minutes daily.
